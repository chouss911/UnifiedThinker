Unified Thinker bridges the reasoning-execution gap with modular upgrades.

Abstract

Unified Thinker is a task-agnostic reasoning architecture designed as a unified planning core for diverse generators. By decoupling the Thinker from the Generator, we enable modular reasoning upgrades without retraining. Our framework anchors high-level intents to pixel-level synthesis via Reinforcement Learning (RL).
